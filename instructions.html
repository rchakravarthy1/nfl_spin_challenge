How To Play

The game is very laid back, and you can choose whatever rules you want, but generally the rules go something like this:

  1. Only one pick can be locked per spin
  2. WRs should be true WR1, WR2, and WR3. Check out the team's depth chart to see their official order.
  3. Only one selection per team
  4. Optionally, use the notes section to remember which players you picked

Common Variations Include:

  1. Any WRs for WR1/2/3
  2. All-Time edition: Any player who played on that team at some point
  3. Active Roster Only: Currently injured players can't be picked
